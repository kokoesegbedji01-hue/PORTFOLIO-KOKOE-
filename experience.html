Investment Thesis

Every experience answered part of the same question.

Night audit taught me customer loyalty and execution. Federal court taught me evidence and risk. EY taught me financial integrity. PwC taught me strategy and communication. Grayscale taught me healthcare business quality. Akoma / Mavros taught me capital formation. Global Africa Capital taught me emerging markets. Together, these experiences shaped how I think about one question:

What makes a business worth investing in?

My answer continues to evolve, but I believe it sits at the intersection of people, financial performance, market opportunity, competitive positioning, execution, and the discipline to ask better questions before making decisions.
