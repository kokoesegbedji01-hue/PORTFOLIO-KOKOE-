The Story Behind My Work

My path has been shaped by curiosity, discipline, service, and a desire to understand how people, businesses, and communities create lasting value.

My interest in business started before I entered finance. During undergrad, I worked overnight as a night auditor in Times Square. Between running the nightly audit, reconciling reports, and checking guests in and out, I became fascinated by how businesses actually operate.

That curiosity eventually led me into private equity, investment research, consulting, cross-border investing, and market research across sectors that interest me: healthcare, real estate, agriculture, sports, and emerging markets.
